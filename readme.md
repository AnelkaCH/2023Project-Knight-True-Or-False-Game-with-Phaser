## Play Online

🔗 https://demons-true-or-false.netlify.app/

## About the Game

In **Demon's Trial**, you play as a knight confronting a powerful demon.

To attack, you must answer a series of true-or-false questions. Every correct answer launches a slash attack against the demon, while incorrect answers are intended to trigger a counterattack from the enemy.

The goal is simple: survive the encounter and defeat the demon before running out of lives.

## Gameplay

* Play as a knight battling a demon
* Answer custom true-or-false questions
* Correct answers damage the demon
* Incorrect answers are intended to damage the player
* Victory screen upon defeating the demon and the final score will be displayed

## Current Status

⚠️ This project contains a known bug.

Originally, incorrect answers were supposed to trigger an attack from the demon and reduce the player's lives. However, due to a bug that appeared when revisiting the project, the game currently treats all answers as correct.

Since this is an older learning project, the issue has been left unresolved for now and serves as a reminder of how much I've learned since building it.
